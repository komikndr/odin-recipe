Caesar salad Dishes

Caesar salad is a dish from italy. It is very good The ingredients are:

    Lettuce
    Egg
    Anchovies
    Cruton
    Salt and paper
While to make a Caesar salad you must
  • Chop lettuce salad to bite sizes
  • Mix egg and achovies
  • Combine chopped lettuce with egg and Anchovies
  • Put freshly bake cruton on the caesar salad
  • Mix well and add salt or pepper
